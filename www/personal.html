I hold a bachelor’s degree in actuarial science and became an Associate of the Society of Actuaries in 2011. Later that year I also became a Member of the American Academy of Actuaries.

From 2005 to 2017 I worked in traditional actuarial roles related to healthcare and group benefits. Since 2018 I have worked as a data scientist at Vericity.

I have always been fascinated by the power of mathematics and computers. These tools allow us to understand the world around us and shape it into the world we need.

My actuarial background provides a strong statistical foundation for understanding modern artificial intelligence. Further study in computing technologies will deepen my ability to combine statistical reasoning with modern computational tools.
